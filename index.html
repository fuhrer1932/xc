What Do We Do Here?

Here at the Infinite Space Academy, we strive to giving education the time of your life. This school takes ordinary civilians aged 10-18 and makes them astronauts, cosmologists and complete professional experts on space itself. Who wouldn't like that? We take our kids on amazing trips at least five times a year into the Solar System to view the amazing creation God's made for us. Occasionally, we even take the kids into deep space, millions of light years into it, and let them write an essay on what they see, what is occurring and how that body in space was made. These trips usually last around 5-8 days before they return home. A few examples of these trips are listed below. Kids and teenagers also stay at our school until Monday – Saturday, and they return home on Sunday and come back on Monday.

Our History:

  • Year 2459 - The Infinite Space Academy was opened by Sir Richard Johnson (First man to land on Jupiter) and was originally first called the "Space Academy".
  • Year 2482 - We officially became an outstanding school.
  • Year 2524 - This is when the great Intergalactic War started, all of our supplies had to go to the army to help fight off the Martians.
  • Year 2538 - The end of the Intergalactic War finished, we lost all of our supplies, and the school was on the verge of bankruptcy, until the Federal Government gave us money to rebuild our school.
  • Year 2541 - The school started to introduced a brand new 6th Form to the school.
  • Year 2559 - The schools 100 year anniversary. To celebrate, we introduced a 6th form.
  • Year 2600 - To mark the new century, the Space Academy changes its name to the Infinite Space Academy.
  • Year 2612 - The Academy introduced trips to other galaxies.
  • Year 2625 - The Infinite Space Academy is officialy voted as the best school in the whole of the Milky Way Galaxy.
  • Year 2632 - The school expanded into several different planets in the Andromeda Galaxy.
  • Year 2659 - Our 200 year anniversary!
