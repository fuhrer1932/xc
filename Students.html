Students

We have a strong tradition of Student Voice activities, which provide opportunities for Pupils to become involved and even direct School life. There are many links here to other School areas, especially Enrichment, PSHE and the house system. The following are the main forums and avenues of Student Voice, but also new projects may be started as initiatives change.

Student Committee

The role of the Committee System is to give Pupils the ownership over key areas of school development and, as such, the Committees running may vary year to year. Student Learning Conference

This is an annual event held every year. Pupils from the School Committees from all year groups meet to discuss and review the learning in general and be consulted on key issues.

A few websites to help students with online bullying or distressing matters.

  • Kooth - A place where you can privatly talk to a councillor about anything that may be upsetting you 24/7.
  • Childline - Get help by talking to a councillor by dialing 0800 1111.
  • Bullying.com - Where you can stand up to bullys, and say saomething or tell someone about it.
  • NSPCC - Free information on How to ignore bullys, and to stand up for what you think is right.
  • Stop Bullying - You can get a free face-to-face conversation with a councillor that helps you deal with bullying.
  • Kids Cape - This website helps you with the tough challenges that you face at home.

All of these websites help with abuse going on at home and verbal abuse as well.
